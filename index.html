Welcome to Dr. Priyanka's Physiotherapy Clinic

Dr. Priyanka Singh (PT) is a Specialist Physical Therapist, having more than 17 years of experience. She offers an innovative approach for women's health through her scientifically researched ways of Physiotherapy. She provides treatment with confidentiality in a peaceful and supportive environment for women.


She provides one-on-one care and customized individually tailored treatment program with lifestyle modifications. It's a specialty clinic for various women's health problems to improve their quality of life by thoroughly evaluating and treating conditions that specifically affect women.

"Her motto is women's health 'by the woman and for the women.'"

She does advance care for women who struggle with Obesity, Irregular Menstruation, PCOS/PCOD, Postpartum Recovery, Arthritis, or Menopause problems through Exercise and Lifestyle changes. She designs treatment plans for each woman's needs, challenges, and goals based on recent evidence through research. She works with women of all ages and abilities to help you stay moving.

How does my treatment help?

It has been proven by many research studies that consistent exercise and weight loss can be helpful in regulating your reproductive hormones. My treatment includes a customized exercise program, personalized diet plan, and lifestyle modifications that address the root cause. This holistic approach helps in managing weight, inducing regular ovulation, regulating periods, and improving overall quality of life.

Menopause & Bone Health

+

"Menopause occurs when a woman stops menstruating for at least a year. During Menopause, reproductive hormones decline which results in bone loss."

Research indicates approximately 1 in 3 women over the age of 50 are affected by Osteoporosis worldwide. Weight-bearing exercises along with a healthy lifestyle play a vital role in strengthening bones.

Not only exercises — Diet and Lifestyle changes are equally important for managing Menopause symptoms and maintaining bone health.
